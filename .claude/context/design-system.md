# Design system

## Tone

The product should feel like a calm, data-dense investor workstation rather than a flashy trading app.

## Theme guidance

- support dark and light mode
- dark mode can be the default
- keep strong contrast for numbers and status indicators

## Semantic color usage

- green: profit, healthy status, positive change, on-target
- red: loss, negative drift, over-budget, overdue attention
- yellow: caution, slight drift, fair valuation
- purple: AI or premium analysis surfaces

## Typography

- headings should be bold and clear
- body text should stay highly readable
- numeric data benefits from a monospace treatment for alignment

## Motion

Use motion to clarify state transitions, not to decorate everything.
Examples:
- route transitions can be gentle
- number updates can animate smoothly
- monthly flow cards can stagger in
- avoid dramatic motion during dense financial review
